Our Mission

Here at EcoPulse, our mission is simple. We hope to inspire people across the world to keep the environment in mind when making purchases,
whether that be while online shopping or shopping in person. At EcoPulse, we believe that making small changes in the way we purchase and
consume items can make a big difference for the future of our planet. We are hoping to start a movement to inspire not only customers but also
other companies to help create a better future for the next generations of people.

"We are the first generation to feel the impact of climate change and the last generation that can do something about it" ~ Barack Obama

Our Commitment To Sustainability

Sustainability is an essential part of our business, as our goal is to deliver eco friendly products and to ultimately help the planet.
Carbon offsetting is something we aim to do at EcoPulse, to minimize any impact on the planet when transporting our products across the
world. We are hoping to expand our warehouses globally to help prevent the need for transporting goods through the air and to help create
more jobs across the world. At EcoPulse, while we mainly use renewable energy sources, when that is not possible we do our best to keep
track of our energy and fuel consumption to offset it through reforestation.

Our Vision

At EcoPulse, our visison for the future is for single use plastic items such as plastic bottles, cups, straws and more will be eliminated
by 2030. We are hoping to see a world without litter scattered everywhere you look, and by selling a variety of reusable, biodegradable and
eco friendly products we hope to make a difference and start a movement. Our vision is big and we are aware that this is not something we can
do alone, hence why we are hoping that customers join us in this movement by trying their best to shop for eco friendly products such as the
ones we sell to try make a better tomorrow for future generations.
